The Power of Creation

As a wife and mother with a full-time career, living with depression is often extremely overwhelming. While there are many treatments, I still haven't found the one that actually works... so I do the best I can with what I've got.


Craft Therapy

An Uncommon Approach

While it is becoming a more wide-spread practice, for some the act of creating things helps soothe the symptoms. My own personal experience is that the act of creating something (and being in control of the creation) is extremely beneficial.

Seykolova's Craft Stash

Something To Consider

My partner is very supportive of my unconventional therapy but, as you might imagine, it created a surplus of handcrafted items in our home that did nothing but take up space. He suggested sellling some items at craft fairs. It seemed like a good idea, so Seykolova's pop-up shop was born.

The Art of Compromise

Talking My Brain Into Proper Pricing

Many of my items are underpriced. I know this and am reminded of it often by friends and family. I'm still working on wrapping my head around charging people for my therapy. I found an app that helps price handmade items and have started using it. Hopefully that and learning a little about marketing will be a good combination.
